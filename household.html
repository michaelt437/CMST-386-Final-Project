This table was constructed without any metal. Even the gears are wooden.

This is a nice little display of craftsmanship. With the goal of using only one material in mind, this table was constructed entirely of wood. It can even extend or detract with the help of wooden gears.

Computer wires and their potential for creating nightmarish messes and frustration are eternal. Luckily, with this new product, your wires and cables can be stored together and out of eye-sight, as well as provide extra storage for your office supplies.
