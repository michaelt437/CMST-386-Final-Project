Algae feeds off CO2 from highway cars

The never ending journey for energy keeps getting more and more interesting. Research into plants as viable alternatives to energy sources is continuously being done. This concept puts CO2 eating algae right above a hotspot - cars.

Sadly, it is only a concept at the moment, and the only installation is located in France. However, this concept is a brilliant advancement in the direction of cleaning out atmosphere. CO2 feeding algae has always been a highly pushed method. As we all know, and for those who don't, plants in general use carbon dioxide for food. They absorb the toxins from the atmosphere and release oxygen into the air.

This algae concept, which can be called a cultivating farm, might even run infinitely. Being about a highway, there shouldn't be any lack of CO2 to consume. An abundance of sunlight is also a plus for these tiny helpers.
