As fast as the speed of an ostrich

We've all, at some point, desired to run faster than the next person - racing your friends during recess, competing against fellow sprinters for the gold, or just merely running from the bad guys. This guy wants to run faster than the fastest land animals of the planet.

Keahi Seymour has invented boots that allows him to run faster than a normal human being. His design imitates the physical structre of an ostrich, specifically their achilles tendons. The design allows him to propel himself more than usual. Currently, his top speed caps at 25 mph. That's pretty fast.

Seymour is currently still working to perfect his boots. His goal is to run as faster, and most likely faster, than the fastest land animals of Earth. The idea is pretty cool. Runners might love the enhanced breeze in their face during their highs. Let's just not imagine the damage that could possibly occur if you were to fall at those speeds.
