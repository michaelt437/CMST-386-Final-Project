Wearable

Monitor your heart rate with this bra.

Personal health has always been important to a lot of us, and lately, there has been many new innovations to help us reach our goals. Riding the wave of heart rate monitoring technology, Victoria's Secret has a new bra.

Obviously, this innovation targets women who want to keep their health in check in more ways than one, or to eliminate other heart rate monitoring gadgets. This bra contains nodes within the fabric that detects the heart rate of the wearer. Pretty awesome if you ask me.

So far, we have had our smart phones keeping track of our health, watches (amid a renewed surge of ideas and concepts), shoes even. Maybe somewhere on the horizon we can get a complete T-shirt, or warmup suit, to keep track of our heart rate or how many calories we're losing.

My all time favorite method remains to be the two-fingers-on-your-wrist (thumb side) method. I know it does not tell you your heart rate, but this discovery amazed me when I was younger.
