Modular iPhone case concept is X-cellent

Some may thing that modular designs, as opposed to ones that are more singular or all-in-one, are not as efficient and run the risk of losing parts. The beauty of modular design though is the customization. This iPhone case lets you attach your phone to all sorts of things.

This concept is so simple, yet the options that it brings is endless. Dubbed the eXo-Skeleton, this iPhone case not only reinforces the phone - force absorption, easier to grip - it takes up minimum space and actually is quite revealing. The case utilizes spring systems to attach to and release the iPhone. On top of that, the case can attach itself to various other objects. This is where the modular aspect comes in. Attaching a clip to the case and you can easily clasp your phone to your belt or pocket. Another clip allows you clasp your phone to your wallet, or make your phone become your new wallet/money clip.

I really like this concept, because I am one who enjoys to have my iPhone in its glorious nakedness. I think the clean, stainless steel, X on the back of it would improve its aesthetic, and it goes nicely with the iPhone's already sleek design.
