Household

Rotating shelf lets you put things up anyway you like.

Here is a neat one. A new Kickstarter campaign has underway for this sweet 360° rotating shelf. You can place these anywhere in the house, rotate them any which angle you want, and place all kinds of books, picture frames, Bluray's, or iPads in them. Maybe iPads should stay closer to the ground...but you can do what you want.

The shelf itself is made of wood and comes in different colors. The product is advertised as a clever way to decorate your rooms and add color and interest at the same time. The rotation of the shelf is controlled by an adjustment system hidden within the shelf.

The greatest thing about this concept is how much customization it allows. Everyone is different, and this product would be a great way for people to express who they are by setting their own angles.

Follow this link to be taken to the Kickstarter page. This is a great concept that when combining different colors and crazy angles will surely add spice to any room. I also can't wait for someone to tell me that my bookshelf is crooked so that I can tell them "No, it's not."
